I am researcher in Machine Intelligence Applied to text based reviews.

Research interests

My main research goal is to develop AI and machine learning solutions for analysing different type of data. I work on machine learning, deep learning and natural language processing to solve problems and develop new technologies for the future text analysis systems.
  • Time-series data processing and pattern analysis
  • Continual learning and semi-supervised learning models
